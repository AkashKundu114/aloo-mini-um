Technology Solutions to Hunger

Technology provides powerful tools to help solve hunger. Innovations in agriculture, logistics, and data analysis are being used to improve food systems worldwide.

1. Drones in Farming

Drones help monitor crop health, irrigation levels, and soil conditions, allowing farmers to use resources more efficiently.

2. AI for Crop Prediction

Artificial Intelligence can analyze weather, soil, and satellite data to predict harvest times and optimize yields.

3. Food Delivery Apps

Apps like Zomato and Uber Eats help connect excess food from restaurants to people in need via donation programs.

4. Data Analytics for Distribution

Organizations use data to track hunger hotspots and distribute food aid more effectively and fairly.
