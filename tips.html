Simple Tips for Students

Every student can play a part in fighting hunger. Here are some practical ways to make a difference:

1. Avoid Food Waste

  • Take only what you can eat at school or home.
  • Store leftovers properly or share food instead of wasting it.

2. Volunteer for a Cause

  • Join local food drives or NGOs working on hunger issues.
  • Help distribute surplus food to those in need.

3. Raise Awareness

  • Talk to classmates about food waste and hunger.
  • Create posters or presentations in school events.
